CONTROLS & TACTICS

MOVEMENT: Arrows / WASD — precise platforming like Prince of Persia.
JUMP: SPACE or W — HOLD longer for higher variable jumps + coyote time + buffer for responsive feel.
CROUCH / ROLL: SHIFT or S — Duck under spinning saws. Hold while moving for a roll slide.
CAPE ATTACK/GLIDE: X — Swing cape/sword to stun enemies and activate distant switches. HOLD X mid-air to GLIDE slowly.
POSSESS (Oddworld style): E near glowing allies or stunned foes — Take control temporarily to solve puzzles, reach levers, cross gaps. Time is limited! Press E again to exit.
CLIMB: Near vertical platform edges press UP/W to shimmy up like classic platformers.
FOCUS SLOW: Q or Z — brief cinematic time-slow for precision jumps/traps (recharges on use).
SWITCHES & LEVERS: Walk close + E or hit with cape to toggle platforms, open doors. Some require orbs or specific paths.
ORBS: Collect ALL courage orbs in each section to power the exit portal. Secrets hidden on alternate routes.
ENEMIES: Jump-stomp grunts when falling. Roll under spinners or stun first with cape.
SECRETS: Hidden orbs, moving lifts and multiple paths reward the brave. Funny non-lethal moments await.

SCORING: Orbs +100–200, defeats +250, secrets +400, time bonuses. Local high scores!
